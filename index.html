Github / Google Scholar / Resume

I am a second-year Ph.D. student advised by Prof. Henry Liu in Michigan Traffic Lab. Before that, I received my bachelor degree from School of Vehicle and Mobility, Tsinghua University in Jul, 2018.

My research interests include traffic modeling, traffic control and management, traffic data mining especially for connected vehicle trajecotry data. I am familiar with optimization, control theory, statistical inference/estimation, and machine learning algorithms.
